Safety & Protection

The Triratna Foundation, Inc. is a reliable wheel of human safety and protection. We represent different cultural backgrounds worldwide as a common standard. We recognize the fundamental dignity and natural rights of all human family members as the foundation of freedom, justice, and peace in the world. All human beings shall enjoy the freedom of speech, expression, belief, and freedom from fear. If a man has no support, as the last option, he revolts against tyranny and oppression; there, the rule of law should protect his rights.

People have rights to their faith in the dignity and worth of the human person and men and women's equal rights. We support the promotion of universal respect for and observance of human rights and fundamental freedoms. Love, compassion, integrity, accountability, justice, initiative, information, quality, inspiration, generosity, culture, faith, spirituality, confidence, clarity, self-discipline, self-respect, role-model, teamwork, others first, and change are Triratna Foundation's reliable wheels to accomplish safety and protection to the entire humankind.

We support UDHR's 30 Articles on Human Rights: All human beings are born free and equal in dignity and rights. They are endowed with reason and conscience and should act towards one another in a spirit of brotherhood (UDHR, Article-1). Everyone has the right to life, liberty, and security of person (UDHR, Article-3). No one shall be subjected to torture or cruel, inhuman, or degrading treatment or punishment (UDHR, Article -5).
Everyone has the right to recognition everywhere as a person before the law (UDHR, Article-6).
