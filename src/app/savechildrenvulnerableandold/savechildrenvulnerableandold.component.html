Save Children, Vulnerable & Old

The children, vulnerable and old, are the most defenseless and always among the first to respond, and their voices being heard the first. We work to take care of their problems to the best we could. They are dying from preventable causes, missing education, suffering from violence, exploitation, neglect, and are vulnerable in emergency circumstances. They need a powerful voice to stand up for them, act on their behalf and make their lives pleasanter across the globe. Triratna Foundation is the voice for these vulnerable through global programs that focus on hunger, clothing, shelter, safety, protection, health, education, emergency, and disaster reliefs.
