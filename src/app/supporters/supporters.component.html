Supporters & Partners

The potential sources of any support such as donations, gifts, bequests, grants, permissions, licenses, and permits to run the activities of Triratna Foundation, Inc are our supporters or partners. They are individuals, monks, devotees, well-wishers, sponsors, donors, businesses, corporations, firms, NGOs, INGOs, Governments, and their Departments, UNO specialized agencies, etc... Triratna Foundation, Inc is a 501(c)(3) corporation. All donations and gifts are deductible to the amount permissible under IRS regulations in the US. The statistics show that thousands of people die every day because of hunger, millions have no clothes, & millions are homeless. Our supporters and partners are the personalities who contribute food, extra pennies, clothes, and other supplies to someone dying of starvation on the street or in a remote village. The donors' generous contribution ensures a smile, and the sufferers' blessings cherish happiness and prosperity to supporters.
