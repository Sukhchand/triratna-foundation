Financial

The Finance Committee shall be responsible for making sure the corporation's financial reports are accurate. It shall also oversee the budget, recordkeeping requirements/ internal controls for funding, including tracks/audits donors, amounts donated, and the final destination of funds and perform other duties like establishing reserve funds, lines of credit, and investments. The fundraising committee shall ensure and contribute well-planned fundraising initiatives for the organization. Besides, this Committee shall identify potential sources of funds, take an active role in enhancing the Board's awareness of fundraising opportunities, explore opportunities for improved public relations and fundraising, and provide an annual review of its fundraising performance plan. The potential sources of funds to run our activities will be individual donations, gifts, bequests, corporate contributions, foundation grants, government grants, contracts, interest or profit from investments, and loans/program-related investments. TRIRATNA FOUNDATION, Inc. is a 501(c)(3) corporation. All donations and gifts are deductible to the amount permissible under IRS regulations.
