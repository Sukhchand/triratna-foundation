Well-Wishers

Anyone who matches the criteria specified below is qualified to sign up as a well-wisher. There is no cost to sign up. We provide equal opportunities to all irrespective of race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, or genetics

Criteria

Anyone who supports our vision and mission and wishes TRIRATNA INTERNATIONAL FOUNDATION, Inc to be the most reliable wheel for peace, prosperity, & happiness for humans and animals globally and be a part to ensure a smile on the face of every suffering being will qualify to sign up as a well-wisher.

Duties

The primary duty of a well-wisher affiliated with TRIRATNA INTERNATIONAL FOUNDATION, Inc is to support and wish for peace, prosperity, & happiness for humans and animals globally and ensure a smile on the face of every suffering being in person or remotely.
