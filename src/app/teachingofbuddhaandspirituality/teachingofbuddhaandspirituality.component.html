Teaching of Buddha & Spirituality

Worship Services

We have a place of worship, routinely scheduled services, and an organized assemblage headquartered in Omaha, USA. It incorporates associated cultures, customs, rituals, and spiritual practices based on Lord Buddha's original teachings and interpreted philosophies with due respect to other living religions. The guiding principles of our activities are three universal truths (nothing is lost in the universe, everything changes, the law of cause and effect), Four noble truths, Eight-fold paths, Triratna, Five precepts of Buddhism, Theory of Middle-path, Forgiveness & Reconciliation, Enlightenment, Reincarnation, Karma, Nirvana, and Non-sectarian view of Buddhism.
