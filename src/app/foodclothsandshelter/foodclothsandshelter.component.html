Food, Cloths & Shelter

Are you hungry, do you have food? Do you have weather appropriate clothes? Do you have a safe place to sleep? These questions might be regarded as not right when you do not see the people of these categories; however, statistics show that millions of people die every year because of hunger, millions have no clothes, & millions are homeless. What if we give our extra food, extra pennies, excess clothes to someone dying of starvation on the street or in a remote village? For the sake of God, please contribute what you can. Their blessings will bring every luck and fortune you have been looking for, for years. God Bless You!
