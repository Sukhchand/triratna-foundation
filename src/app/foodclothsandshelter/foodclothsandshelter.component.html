Food, Cloths & Shelter

The immediate basic needs of humans are food, clothing, and shelter. The statistics show that millions of people die every year because of hunger, millions have no clothes, & millions are homeless. What if we give our extra food, extra pennies, excess clothes to someone dying of starvation on the street or in a remote village. Their blessings and wishes will bring cherished happiness and prosperity to you. Your generous contribution will ensure a smile on the face of suffering beings.
